BrightLife prod. Q&A
What are your goals?
Our goals are quite well summarized with 'making the world a better place by providing it with green, eco-friendly and affordable energy'. If you want a more in-depth explanation, read through our Corporate Identity Document.
The product I want is sold out. What should I do now?
Just wait. We are a busy company with a lot of work to do, so if an item is not available at the moment, it certainly will be again in a short period of time. No need to panic!
What are your job requirements if I want to apply at your company?
We don't have any requirements, just wishes. For one, you should be interested in the work you are doing and you also should be able to work well together with your co-workers. Teamwork is everything!
What partner companies are you working with?
We are currently working with a number of companies, including renewable energy providers, manufacturing companies, and other sustainability-focused businesses. We are always looking for new partners and collaborators to help us continously grow and provide the best products and services to you. Our current partners include:
  • Solar Town, a big company rooted in Albuquerque that provides solar energy solutions such as solar roofs
  • GreenGlow, a company that manufactures eco-friendly products
  • The Eco Friend, a company that offers sustainability consulting services
  • Skylight Innovations, a company that specializes in energy efficient windows and doors
  • OceanLife, a company that provides ocean conservation solutions
  • Trash Shop, an online shop that provides waste management solutions
  • Planet Green, an environmental engineering firm that provides sustainable solutions for businesses
  • Save the Planet, a non-profit organization that focuses on environmental education and advocacy
How do I know if the products I am buying are eco-friendly?
All of our products are certified eco-friendly and all the materials used in production are certified by the relevant authorities. We also have a sustainability page on our website, which provides more detailed information about the materials used in our products.
What is the return policy for your products?
We offer a 30-day return policy for all our products. You can return the product for a full refund within the 30-day period. Please make sure to read through our return policy before making a purchase.
Do you offer any discounts for your products?
Yes, we do offer discounts for our products. We have special offers and promotions that are available throughout the year. You can also get discounts when you buy in bulks.
What payment methods do you accept?
We acept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. You can also use Bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies for payment.
What is Calendum?
Calendum is a company founded by Torben H. that produces digital calenders with a lot of useful information. It is a part of the Brightlife prod. family. We believe that calenders should be more than just a way to keep track of dates, but also a way to organize your life and stay on top of important tasks.
What is visPluvium?
visPluvium is a company founded by Vincent H. that produces energy generators for homes that work by rain in the gutter. It is a part of the Brightlife prod. family. Our generators are designed to be eco-friendly and energy efficient, and they are a great way to reduce your energy costs while also helping to reduce your carbon footprint.
What makes Brightlife prod. different from other companies?
We are a unique company that combines two different aspects of sustainability - energy efficiency and home organization. We believe in making the world a better place by providing it with green, eco-friendly energy, while also helping people to stay organized and productive. We are committed to providing high-quality products and excellent customer service.
Who are the founders of Brightlife?
Vincent H. and Torben H. are the two founders of Brightlife prod. Vincent H. is the founder of visPluvium, and Torben H. is the founder of Calendum. They both have a passion for sustainability and have been working together to create Brightlife prod. since 2018.
How did you come up with the name?
The name “Brightlife” was inspired by the idea of providing energy solutions and helping people to live their best life. We wanted to create a name that was simple, memorable and that could represent our commitment to sustainability and making the world a better place. It is made up of two parts - “Bright” for energy solutions and “Life” for living your optimal life.
