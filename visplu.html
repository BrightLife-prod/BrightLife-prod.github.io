Eco-friendly energy, inspired by nature

How it works

The visPluvium turbine uses the potential energy of rainwater on your roof to generate eco-friendly energy. The water enters the turbine, which subsequently transforms this kinetic energy into electricity which you can then use in your home to power numerous devices.
